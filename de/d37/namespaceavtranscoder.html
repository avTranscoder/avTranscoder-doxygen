Enum to set a policy of how we manage the transcode in case of several streams. eProcessMethodShortest: stop transcode at the end of the shortest stream. eProcessMethodLongest: stop transcode at the end of the longest stream. eProcessMethodBasedOnStream: stop transcode at the end of an indicated stream (.
